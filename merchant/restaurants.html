Best Credit Card for Restaurants & Dining

Dining out is one of the most rewarding categories for credit cards — literally. Many cards offer 3-5% back on restaurants, including takeout, delivery apps, and bars. With the average household spending $250-400/month dining out, that's $90-240/year in cash back.

Restaurant rewards cover more than you'd think: sit-down dining, fast food, coffee shops, food delivery (DoorDash, Uber Eats), and bars all code as dining. Some cards even include streaming services in their dining categories.

The best strategy depends on your dining volume. Heavy diners should use a dedicated 4-5% dining card. Occasional diners do fine with a 3% general bonus or 2% flat rate. Never charge more than you can pay in full.
