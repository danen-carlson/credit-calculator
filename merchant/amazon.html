Best Credit Card for Amazon Purchases

Amazon is the king of online shopping, from everyday essentials to Prime Day hauls and Subscribe & Save deliveries. The right credit card can turn your cart into serious cash back or travel points. Focus on cards with bonus categories for online shopping — your top Amazon spend category.

With average households spending $500+ monthly on Amazon (essentials, gifts, electronics), a 5% bonus card could earn $300/year in rewards. Stack with Amazon deals, Prime benefits, and promotional financing for maximum value. Avoid interest by paying in full — rewards only beat interest if you do.

Key factors for Amazon: online shopping bonuses, rotating quarterly categories (Amazon often Q4), no foreign transaction fees for international sellers, and purchase protection. Prime members get extra perks with store cards, but general cards offer more flexibility.
