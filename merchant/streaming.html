Best Credit Card for Streaming Services

The average household pays $50-80/month for streaming — Netflix, Spotify, Disney+, Hulu, YouTube Premium, and more. It adds up to $600-960/year. The right card can earn 3-6% back on these subscriptions.

Several cards offer bonus rewards specifically for streaming services, often categorized under entertainment or digital subscriptions. This includes video streaming, music, gaming subscriptions (Xbox Game Pass, PlayStation Plus), and news subscriptions.

Since streaming charges are recurring and predictable, they're perfect for category optimization. Keep a dedicated streaming card on file for all your subscriptions and watch the rewards compound. Even at $65/month, a 6% rate earns $47/year — essentially a free month of streaming.
