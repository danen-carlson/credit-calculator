Best Credit Card for Gas Stations

Gas is a non-negotiable expense for most households — $150-300/month for commuters. The right card can earn 3-5% back at the pump, turning essential spending into $50-180/year in rewards.

Gas station rewards come in three flavors: dedicated gas cards (3-5% at gas stations), rotating category cards (gas often in quarterly bonuses), and flat-rate cards (2% everywhere including gas). EV charging stations typically code the same as gas stations.

Pro tip: Gas stations sometimes code convenience store purchases differently from fuel. If the station has a convenience store code, you may earn the gas bonus on snacks too. Pay in full monthly to maximize net rewards.
