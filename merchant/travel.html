Best Credit Card for Travel & Airlines

Travel rewards are the most lucrative category in credit cards — premium cards offer 3-5x points on travel, plus lounge access, trip insurance, and no foreign transaction fees. Whether you fly monthly or once a year, the right travel card can save hundreds.

Travel cards shine because points are worth more when redeemed for travel. A 3x travel card earning 1.5 cents/point effectively gives you 4.5% back. Plus, many cards offer travel credits, TSA PreCheck reimbursement, and airport lounge access that add $200-500/year in value.

The catch: premium travel cards charge $95-550 annual fees. Run the math: if the benefits exceed the fee, it's worth it. A $95 card earning 3% on $3,000/year in travel = $90 back — nearly the fee. A $250 card needs $8,000+ in travel spending to justify itself, unless you value perks like lounges.
